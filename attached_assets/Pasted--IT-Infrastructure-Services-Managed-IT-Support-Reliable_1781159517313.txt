Q1. What are Managed IT Services?
Managed IT Services means outsourcing the day-to-day management of your IT infrastructure — monitoring, maintenance, support, and security — to a specialist partner like Cybaem Tech for a fixed monthly fee. You get an entire IT department's capability at a fraction of the cost of hiring in-house.
Q2. What is included in an IT AMC?
Our Annual Maintenance Contracts cover preventive maintenance, scheduled health checks, hardware and software maintenance, security updates, performance tuning, and priority support for desktops, laptops, servers, networks, firewalls, and storage systems.
Q3. Do you provide both remote and onsite support?
Yes. Most issues are resolved instantly through secure remote support, and our engineers provide onsite assistance whenever physical intervention is needed.
Q4. Can you work alongside our existing IT team?
Absolutely. Our Co-Managed IT model is designed exactly for this — we supplement your internal team with 24x7 monitoring, specialist skills (security, cloud, networking), and extra capacity during peak demand.
Q5. How quickly do you respond to issues?
Response times are defined in your SLA based on issue priority. Critical incidents receive immediate attention with continuous escalation until resolved. Typical response for critical issues is within 15–30 minutes.
Q6. Which locations do you serve?
We are headquartered in Pune (Hinjawadi) and serve clients across India, with remote-first support that extends to international clients as well.
Q7. How much do Managed IT Services cost?
Pricing depends on the number of users, devices, servers, and the service level you need. We offer per-user and per-device monthly pricing models. Contact us for a free assessment and a customized quote — most clients save 30–40% compared to in-house IT costs.
